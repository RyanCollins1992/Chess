Privacy Policy — MentorChess

Last updated: 17 July 2026

MentorChess is a chess training app. This page explains what data the app touches and where it goes.

No account, no server

MentorChess doesn't have user accounts, sign-in, or a backend server operated by us. There is nothing for us to collect, because there is nowhere for your data to go except your own device.

What's stored, and where

Everything the app remembers about you — your ELO rating, progress/XP, spaced-repetition schedule, settings, and any games you import — is stored locally in your browser or device's own storage (localStorage). It never leaves your device and is never sent to us. Clearing your browser data, uninstalling the app, or using the app's own "reset all progress" option deletes it permanently and irreversibly — we have no copy to restore it from.

Third-party services the app talks to directly

Some features make requests directly from your device to third-party services, on your instruction (e.g. entering a username or pasting a link). We don't sit in the middle of these requests and don't receive a copy of them:

  • Chess.com Published-Data API — when you import your own games or scout an opponent, your device fetches publicly available game data directly from Chess.com for the username you provide. See Chess.com's privacy policy.
  • Lichess.org API — when you paste a Lichess game link for review, your device fetches that public game's data directly from Lichess. See Lichess's privacy policy.
  • Pollinations.ai — the AI Coach feature sends your message and relevant game/position context directly to Pollinations.ai's public API to generate a reply. See Pollinations.ai for their handling of that data.

MentorChess is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Chess.com, Lichess, or Pollinations.ai.

Analytics, tracking, and ads

MentorChess uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how the app is used — page visits, active users, and general usage patterns. It does not run at all until you explicitly accept the cookie-consent banner shown on your first visit; if you reject it, or don't respond, no analytics script loads and no data is sent. Your choice is remembered on your device and can be changed at any time in Settings → About & Licenses → Analytics.

Google Analytics may set cookies and collect information such as your approximate location (derived from IP address), device/browser type, and which pages you visit. It does not receive your ELO rating, progress, games, or any of the app data described above — that stays local as explained in "What's stored, and where." See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google itself handles this data.

MentorChess does not use any advertising SDKs.

Children's privacy

MentorChess does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children, since it doesn't collect personal information at all — everything stays on-device as described above.

Changes to this policy

If what the app does with data changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
